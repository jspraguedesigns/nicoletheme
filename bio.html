About Me

My name is Nicole and I am a professional wedding and portrait photographer based in Mount Laurel, New Jersey.

Photography is my life, my passion and my love (second to my children and husband, of course!)

I have been photographing couples and families for over ten years and every shoot brings something new. I love getting to know my clients’ stories and painting their unique personalities from behind the lens.

What I’ll bring to your wedding and portrait session: enthusiasm, my geeky love of all things wedding, baby, and simply beautiful photography. Your photographs are just as important to me as it is to you.

When I am not spending time with my clients, I can be found playing board games with my children and husband or exploring the area with our rescued puppy. Random fact about me, I really love cheese. All types and always find a way to add it to my plate.
